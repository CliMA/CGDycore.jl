\begin{align*}
\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}  & = -\frac{\partial u f_1}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial v f_1}{\partial y}\\
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t}  & = -\frac{\partial \rho f_2}{\partial x}\\
\frac{\partial v}{\partial t}  & = -\frac{\partial \rho f_2}{\partial y}
\end{align*}

where $f_1$ and $f_2$ are functions depending on $x$ and $y$.
